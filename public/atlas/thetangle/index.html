The Tangle

Nobody realised what Captain Green really did. They saw a couple square blocks become wild, fed by Wyld Magic and insanity. That wasn’t true, it was largner, much larger. The small space seen became a larger space of several square block, each holding many times what it would have held for physical space. The blocks that vanished were swallowed up by the plants that filled it, tens of years of nature taking over compressed into a short perioud. The sleepers, well they diddn’t realize a thing.

Mages and Garou, however, were a different story. Most mages ventured in a short ways, then were driven back by what took over the spaces. Black Spiral Dancers, recoiled from the strong presence of the Wyld. Void Engineers cordoned things off and sought to contain any that ventured in after the first few days.

Inside the zone though, there are still things to be found, if you know where to look.
