{{< /center >}}

For most people, what goes down the drains never comes back. In the City, what goes down the drain can come back to haunt you or crawl out to hunt you. Gators may be the least of your worries.

The drains in the City were inspired by many sources. Stories, the Paris Catacombs, urban legends about New York, and a wastewater management class in college. IT also played a large part. 

It is a place that is inhabited and influenced by many beings and organizations. It's also a dumping ground. All you would expect from a big city, plus industry, plus World of Darkness industry, barrabi, Black Spiral Dancers, Banes, and many other things. 

It is not a place Public Works likes to go. Especially not without a lot of lights and noise. Most things, let Public Works be. Most.
