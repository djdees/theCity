This is a place that has lived in my mind for years, rent free. It's ebbed and flowed, but has always been there. It's returned at odd moments, late at night or when my mind wanders. 

Urb-ex, various readings, one of those flashes where you know you've been here before.

There's more here than there was when it was live. Not all from the original is documented yet - I'll be trying to fix that. However, there's also 20+ years of new things that have crept in.

I couldn't run it again. Not in the same way or with the same cast. It's slept, but it's not dead - far from it.
