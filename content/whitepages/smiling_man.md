An extra insane BSD with a very truncated muzzle and mouth that appears to be smiling. Each tooth is laced with veins of silver.

He hungers and hunts, but has been driven from one of the local packs.

He has an irrational fear of chalked or spray painted human outlines

He lives in the abandoned Shermer High School, one tactic he uses is to put out advertisements for raves and then picks off one or two attendees.
