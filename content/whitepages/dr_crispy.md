Regularly seen in the area around the [Mission of the Helping Hand](/yellowpages/helpinghand/), he consistenly claims that at one time he made the best fried chicken when he was a cook in New Orleans.

Those that have known him for years know better. He was once covered in lighter fluid and lit on fire. Regardless, he pays attention to what's going on and, if he likes you, he'll share what he sees.
